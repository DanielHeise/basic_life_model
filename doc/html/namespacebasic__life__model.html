enum basic_life_model::TRANSITIONS

life transitions of an organism

Enumerator
BIRTH 

an organism is born into an empty cell with exactly three living neighbors

SURVIVAL 

an organism with either two or three living neighbors survives from one generation to the next

DEATH 

an organism with four or more neighbors dies from overcrowding. an organism with fewer than two neighbors dies from loneliness
